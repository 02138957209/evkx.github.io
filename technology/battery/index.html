EV Batteries

EV batteries are responsible for storing the energy that powers the vehicle's electric motor, and they play a critical role in determining an EV's performance, range, and overall cost.

In this article, we will explore the different types of EV batteries, how they work, their advantages and disadvantages, and the latest developments in EV battery technology.

By the end of this article, you will better understand the current state and prospects of EV batteries and their role in the transition to a more sustainable transportation system.

  • Battery Pack & configuration - 400 volts vs. 800. Cell to module vs. Cell to pack, and more.
  • Battery management system - The brain of the battery system
  • Thermal management - Why is thermal management essential for performance, and how does it work
